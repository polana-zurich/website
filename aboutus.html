About us

Siema! We are POLANA (Polish Academic Network Abroad). We are an association that brings together Polish students and students interested in Polish culture in Zurich and the surrounding area to celebrate Polish traditions and get to know each other. From movie nights and hikes to traditional Christmas Eve dinners, there is something for everyone at POLANA.
