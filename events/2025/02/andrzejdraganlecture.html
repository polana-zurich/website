Quantum Mechanics vs. Relativity: Bridging the Divide

25.02.2025, 17:15

HG G 3, Rämistrasse 101, 8092 Zürich

We are thrilled to invite you to an exclusive lecture by Prof. Andrzej Dragan – a distinguished Polish physicist, professor at the University of Warsaw, and a pioneer in quantum physics research.

Prof. Dragan is not only a highly respected scientist but also a major science communication star. His unique approach to explaining complex physics concepts has gained immense recognition – his lectures and videos on YouTube have reached up to 10 million views! With his unconventional style and sharp wit, he inspires people worldwide to dive deeper into the mysteries of science.

During this lecture, Prof. Dragan will explore the relationship between quantum mechanics and relativity – two fundamental yet seemingly contradictory pillars of modern physics. He will discuss how these theories intertwine and what their connection means for our understanding of the universe.

This event is a unique opportunity to explore cutting-edge physics concepts with one of the most prominent science communicators.

The lecture will be held in English. We warmly invite everyone to join, regardless of whether they are from ETH, another university, or not studying at all.
